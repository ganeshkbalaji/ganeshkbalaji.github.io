Intergalactic VR

Intergalactic VR is a virtual reality experience for smartphone web browsers and Google Cardboard. The experience takes you on a virtual reality tour of a facility with a car garage, and into space displaying an audio visualizer completely created with 3D objects. Intergalactic VR was engineered in JavaScript with Three.js, WebGL, and Web Audio API.
